 #### 24.1.68. `@PostLoad`

    <div class="paragraph">

    The [`@PostLoad`](http://docs.oracle.com/javaee/7/api/javax/persistence/PostLoad.html) annotation is used to specify a callback method that fires after an entity is loaded.

    </div>
