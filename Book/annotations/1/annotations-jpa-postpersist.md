 #### 24.1.69. `@PostPersist`

    <div class="paragraph">

    The [`@PostPersist`](http://docs.oracle.com/javaee/7/api/javax/persistence/PostPersist.html) annotation is used to specify a callback method that fires after an entity is persisted.
