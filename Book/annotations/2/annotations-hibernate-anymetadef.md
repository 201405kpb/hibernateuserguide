#### 24.2.3. `@AnyMetaDef`

<div class="paragraph">

The [`@AnyMetaDef`](https://docs.jboss.org/hibernate/orm/5.2/javadocs/org/hibernate/annotations/AnyMetaDef.html) annotation is used to provide metadata about an `@Any` or `@ManyToAny` mapping.
