#### 24.1.74. `@PreUpdate`

<div class="paragraph">

The [`@PreUpdate`](http://docs.oracle.com/javaee/7/api/javax/persistence/PreUpdate.html) annotation is used to specify a callback method that fires before an entity is updated.

</div>
<div class="paragraph">
