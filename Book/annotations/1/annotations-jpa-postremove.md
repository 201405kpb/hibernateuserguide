 #### 24.1.70. `@PostRemove`

    <div class="paragraph">

    The [`@PostRemove`](http://docs.oracle.com/javaee/7/api/javax/persistence/PostRemove.html) annotation is used to specify a callback method that fires after an entity is removed.

    </div>
