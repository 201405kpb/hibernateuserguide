#### 24.2.38. `@Immutable`

<div class="paragraph">

The [`@Immutable`](https://docs.jboss.org/hibernate/orm/5.2/javadocs/org/hibernate/annotations/Immutable.html) annotation is used to specify that the annotated entity, attribute, or collection is immutable.
