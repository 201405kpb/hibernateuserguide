#### 24.2.6. `@BatchSize`

<div class="paragraph">

The [`@BatchSize`](https://docs.jboss.org/hibernate/orm/5.2/javadocs/org/hibernate/annotations/BatchSize.html) annotation is used to specify the size for batch loading the entries of a lazy collection.
