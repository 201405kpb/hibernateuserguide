 #### 24.1.73. `@PreRemove`

    <div class="paragraph">

    The [`@PreRemove`](http://docs.oracle.com/javaee/7/api/javax/persistence/PreRemove.html) annotation is used to specify a callback method that fires before an entity is removed.

    </div>
